Privacy Policy

Heads and Tails Haulage Pty Ltd (ABN 36 657 016 496), 454 Mount Barker Road, Crafers West SA 5152 ("we", "us") is committed to handling your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles.

What we collect and why

  • Contact and booking details (name, company, ABN, email, phone, site suburb) — to process and manage your hire.
  • Driver licence details and an uploaded copy/photo of your licence — to verify you are legally entitled to drive our truck (MR class or higher) and to identify hirers, as required by our insurance and heavy vehicle law obligations.
  • Operator competency declarations — to meet our work health and safety duties as a supplier of plant.
  • Payment details — processed by Stripe; we never see or store your full card number.

Storage and retention

Licence uploads are stored in a secured, access-controlled drive and retained for no longer than 7 years after your last hire (to cover claim and limitation periods), then securely destroyed. You may request access to, or correction or earlier deletion of, your information at any time by emailing headsandtails@gmail.com (subject to our legal retention obligations).

Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We may disclose it to our insurers, payment processor, debt collectors, police or government authorities where reasonably necessary — for example, nominating the responsible driver for a traffic infringement issued during your hire.

GPS / telematics

Our equipment may be fitted with GPS tracking. Location data is collected during hire periods for security, recovery and billing purposes only.
